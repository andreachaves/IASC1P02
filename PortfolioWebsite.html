About

In this page I demonstrate my development from the first page through my artwork, my prototyping, in my essays and in my photography. I use skills that I learned this year through the readings and lectures such as the use of colour theory in design, the understanding of immersive fallacy in my own way, and the understanding of collaberation and group projects. This semester may have had less work but it has had more developing and learning. It has allowed me to grow as an artist and decide what I want to concentrate on during my time in interactive and arts.
