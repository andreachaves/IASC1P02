About

Above are all designs, prototypes, websites, and drawings that I have made up this semester for classes or out of school needs. The top row includes different shirt designs that I came up with for one of the biggest junior squash tournaments in Canada held at the White Oaks Resort and Spa here in Niagara-on-the-Lake. The second row consists of graffiti drawings and designs I worked on for a Labour studies course (LABR2P91) held here at Brock. The third row consists of a website I created, a web page prototype, a visual presentation, and a website redesign prototype all made up for courses in my classes this semester. The fourth row consists of part of the Axure team project (sign in page, connect page, live feed page) for this course as well as the use of Slack. Finally, the bottom page consists of out of class drawings that I do free-hand. They are of several rappers either that I have chosen to draw or have been asked to. All of these designs are created by me and are things I have enjoyed working on and will continue to do and expand on as the year goes on.
