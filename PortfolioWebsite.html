About

My portfolio consists of designs using Photoshop, InDesign, Assembly and others alike. It ranges from designs, to drawings, to prototypes, to photographs to show my range of abilities. A few of the works shown were made for organizations where the others were either assigments or created on my own time for me. I am very hands on when I work and enjoy all platforms to work on. I often use different applications to make any sorts of design as I enjoy learning new ways of creating my work as well as different techniques and methods to further expand my creativity. -Andrea Chaves
