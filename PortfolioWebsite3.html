About

This page shows my work and progress throughout this year in developing art for a video game we are making in IASC3F02 called Spooky Beatz. The game is a 2D classic graphic style game that resembles the 16 bit era. On this page you can see my rough drafts and prototyping for the characters, refrence images I used, development of sprites and the moving characters themselves. These are just some of the designs and characters I made for the game but you can see throughout the game devlopment stages I made changes, adding more detail and including more complex characters and ideas. Spooky Beatz is a classic style game with refrences from today's world, like Drake, to add a little new age into the game.
